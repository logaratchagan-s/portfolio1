A little about me

L. — KPR

I started out taking apart old radios, which is probably why I still think of models as something you build, not just something you train. A system should be legible enough that the next person — often future me — can open it up and understand the joints.

I'm in my final year of a B.E. in Computer Science, specializing in AI and Machine Learning, at KPR College. Most of my coursework and side projects sit at the intersection of ML and software engineering: getting a model out of a notebook and into something with an interface, a deploy pipeline, and a person on the other end of it.

Outside of class I tinker with small electronics and build side projects for fun. Open to internships and remote collaboration.
